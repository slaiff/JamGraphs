Welcome to JamGraphs

JamGraph is a user-friendly Spotify Visualization tool that allows users to connect their spotify account and view graphs generated based on their listening habits.

About JamGraphs

JamGraphs is a web application designed for the Cs 290 Final project at Oregon State University. It allows users to connect their spotify account and generate visualizations, and share them on public feed.
